Abstract摘要 · academic-facing version

Problem. Modern aesthetic theory often treats beauty as either a property of objects, a disinterested judgment, or a culturally trained taste, but it rarely distinguishes between direct bodily aesthetic events and consciousness-mediated aesthetic registration. This ambiguity makes it difficult to explain why artworks and natural objects can feel disinterested at the surface while still reflecting value, memory, education, culture, and self-positioning. Approach. Cognitive Aesthetics (CA) proposes a conceptual distinction between bodily resonance-events, handled by Three-Modal Aesthetics (TMA), and cognitive-refraction events, handled by CA. Within CA, the earlier term "美学直觉" is retired and replaced by a more precise structure: 艺术美直觉 for human-made works, 自然美直觉 for natural objects, and 审美直觉 as their umbrella term. Contribution. The framework offers five axioms, six corollaries, a two-layer interest principle, a revised diagnosis of Kant's disinterested pleasure, and an applied route toward aesthetic diagnostics, aesthetic pedagogy, and aesthetic counseling.

问题。现代美学常把美理解为对象属性、无利害判断或文化训练出的品味,但很少区分「身体直接审美事件」与「意识中介审美指认」。因此,它难以解释为什么艺术品与自然对象在表面层可以呈现为无利害,却在结构层仍携带价值、记忆、教育、文化与自我位置。方法。认知美学(CA)区分由三模态美学(TMA)处理的身体共振事件,与由 CA 处理的认知折射事件。CA 内部撤回早期术语「美学直觉」,改为更精确的三分结构:面向人造作品的艺术美直觉、面向自然对象的自然美直觉,以及二者合称的审美直觉。贡献。本文建立五公理、六推论、双层利害原则、康德「无利害愉悦」的分层诊断,并打开审美诊断学、审美训练学与审美咨询三条应用路径。

Cognitive Aesthetics studies consciousness's registration of beauty in non-subject objects. Non-subject objects = objects that cannot resonate at the same vibrational layer as the observer:

  • Natural objects — landscapes, plants, animals, astronomical phenomena
  • Any non-subject existence registered by consciousness as aesthetic

认知美学研究意识对非主体对象进行美的指认。非主体对象 = 不能与观看者同层共振的对象:人造作品 / 自然对象 / 任何非主体存在被意识当作审美对象。

Dual-Axis Mutual Exclusivity Axiom双轴互斥公理
An aesthetic event occurs either in bodily contact (TMA, direct event), or in cognitive refraction (CA, via aesthetic intuition), or both simultaneously (trans-axis). Resonance-beauty and artistic-beauty are structurally irreducible — different mechanisms entirely.
审美事件,要么发生在身体接触层(TMA · 直接事件),要么发生在认知折射层(CA · 经审美直觉),要么两者并存(跨轴现象)。共振美与艺术美在结构上不可化约——是完全不同的运作机制。

Corollary · CA · 1
Artistic Beauty = Cognitive Refraction toward Human-Made Works艺术美 = 对人造作品的认知美
Artistic beauty arises through cognitive refraction directed at human-made works (paintings, sculptures, music, literature, films, architecture, design). The work is the trigger, not the source; the source of beauty is the structure of the observer's cognition.
艺术美归属于对人造作品的认知折射。作品是触发器,不是美的来源;美的来源是观看者的认知层结构。

Corollary · CA · 2
Natural Beauty = Cognitive Refraction toward Natural Objects (N-α)自然美 = 对自然对象的认知美
Natural beauty arises through cognitive refraction directed at natural objects (landscapes, plants, animals, celestial events). Natural objects do not initiate resonance response — therefore the human "sense of beauty" toward nature is a cognitive refraction event, structurally isomorphic with artistic beauty. The only distinction lies in object-type: human-made vs natural.
自然美归属于对自然对象的认知折射。自然对象本身不发起共振响应,人对自然的"美感"是认知层折射事件,结构与艺术美同型。区别仅在对象类型。
This corollary extends the P1 path globally and denies Kant's central claim that "natural beauty is the paradigm of pure disinterested aesthetics."

Objection 1 · Kant
Does CA caricature Kant?
Risk. A Kant scholar may say CA treats "disinterested pleasure" too quickly. Repair. The V0.3.1 version should state that CA is not dismissing Kant's phenomenological observation; it accepts the surface phenomenon and rejects only the structural extension from surface disinterest to structural disinterest.
风险。康德研究者可能认为 CA 过快处理"无利害愉悦"。修复。V0.3.1 应明确:CA 不否定康德的现象学观察,而是接受表面现象、拒绝从表面无利害延伸到结构无利害。
Objection 2 · Overclaim
Is "all aesthetic events involve interest" too strong?
Risk. The universal interest principle may sound unfalsifiable. Repair. Define interest minimally as any value, attention, memory, identity, bodily, cultural, conceptual, or motivational stake that shapes the judgment. Then distinguish this from crude utility or explicit desire.
风险。全域利害原则可能显得不可证伪。修复。把利害最低限度定义为任何塑造判断的价值、注意、记忆、身份、身体、文化、概念或动机位置,并把它与粗糙功利和显性欲望区分开。
Objection 3 · Empirical Scope
Does CA make untested psychological claims?
Risk. Terms like "100–300ms" and "diagnostic signal" invite empirical scrutiny. Repair. Treat them as testable hypotheses or move them into an Applied CA / Future Work section. The core theory only requires pre-reflective compression, not a fixed time window.
风险。"100–300ms"与"诊断信号"会触发经验审查。修复。把它们标为可测试假设,或移入应用 CA / 后续研究。核心理论只需要"前反思压缩",不需要固定毫秒窗口。
Objection 4 · TMA Boundary
Can human beauty ever be cognitive rather than bodily?
Reply. Yes, a human can be treated as image, memory, performance, photograph, persona, or artwork-like object. In such cases, CA may co-occur. But live human-to-human beauty in the primary sense remains TMA resonance. This is a trans-axis case, not a collapse of the two axes.
回应。可以。人可以被当作图像、记忆、表演、照片、人格或类作品对象来处理,这时 CA 可以并存。但活的人对人的一阶美感仍是 TMA 共振。这是跨轴,不是两轴坍缩。
