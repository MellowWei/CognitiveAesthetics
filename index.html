AbstractProblem / Approach / Contribution

Abstract摘要 · 投稿层压缩

Problem. Classical aesthetics often describes beauty as disinterested or immediate, while cognitive approaches risk reducing aesthetic experience to heuristics, preference, or cultural training. This leaves a gap between surface aesthetic verdict and the deeper structure that makes such verdicts possible. Approach. Cognitive Aesthetics defines aesthetic intuition as a compressed rapid cognitive judgment. It distinguishes artistic-beauty intuition, natural-beauty intuition, and aesthetic intuition as an umbrella term; it also differentiates unidirectional consciousness mediation from TMA’s bidirectional body vibration. Contribution. The discipline offers a layered diagnosis of Kant’s disinterest, reframes natural beauty as explainable within cognitive beauty under the N-α lock, and establishes a sibling-position constellation with Three-Modal Aesthetics. It is not neuroaesthetics, not Kant commentary, and not a loose combination of “art studies” and “cognitive aesthetics.”

问题。传统美学常把美描述为无利害或即时的，而认知路径又容易把审美经验降为启发式判断、偏好或文化训练。这使“表层审美 verdict”与其背后的认知结构之间出现缺口。方法。认知美学将审美直觉定义为一种压缩的快速认知判断，并区分艺术美直觉、自然美直觉与作为伞概念的审美直觉；同时区分单向意识中介与 TMA 的双向身体振动。贡献。本学科对 Kant 的无利害判断提出层级诊断，在 N-α 落锁下将自然美解释为认知美的一个低层结构，并与 Three-Modal Aesthetics 建立兄弟位态。它不是神经美学，不是康德文本注释，也不是“艺术学 + 认知美学”的松散合订。

EcologyTMA sibling

Ecology Position生态归位 · 与 TMA 的兄弟位态

Cognitive Aesthetics and Three-Modal Aesthetics form a sibling-position constellation. CA handles the mediated aesthetic event in which the subject approaches the object through cognitive refraction and consciousness mediation. TMA handles the immediate bodily-aesthetic event in which beauty, handsomeness, coolness, cuteness, or related modal forces operate as direct or quasi-direct body vibration.

认知美学与 Three-Modal Aesthetics 构成兄弟位态。CA 处理主体通过认知折射与意识中介接触对象的审美事件；TMA 处理美、帅、酷、可爱等模态力量以直接或准直接身体振动运行的审美事件。

生态锁：CA 与 TMA 共生互惠但非本体必然捆绑。任一方可独立成立；若缺少另一方，美学全貌会变窄，但不会导致单站本体失效。
